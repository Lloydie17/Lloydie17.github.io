About this activity

This is our second activity, our task was to create a website called mini songbook. This activity is to list our favorites artist/bands that we listen, my goals for this activity is to help people with music. In my mini songbook I listed the bands and how their song will help you to overcome your problem or the challenges you've face right now.

I created this using HTML with notepad++ as my editor. Since this was already our second activity and after studying CSS I finally applied what I have learned with my self-study, not just CSS but also HTML. Through this activity I was able to helped one of my friend, he asked for my works because he want to get some ideas. Then after that he thanked me.

From the first activity I did learned from my mistakes because I can see that my works are getting better. In my experience as I was making this website it helps me and motivate me to become a Web Developer.
