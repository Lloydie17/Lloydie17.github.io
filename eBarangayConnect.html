Visit the demo website

Overview

eBarangayConnect is an innovative platform designed to enhance barangay operations by providing modernized and efficient solutions. It offers essential online services such as barangay certificates, clearances, and other documentation, making these processes faster and more accessible for residents. The platform also includes robust resident tracking features, enabling barangay officials to identify and reach out to individuals who may require special attention, such as bedridden senior citizens or those unaware of ongoing assistance programs. Moreover, eBarangayConnect fosters improved communication within the community through automated email notifications. These notifications ensure residents are well-informed about barangay activities, events, and emergency alerts, creating a more transparent and engaged community. By streamlining operations and enhancing service delivery, eBarangayConnect significantly contributes to improving the quality of life for all residents, particularly those in need of special assistance.
